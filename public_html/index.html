Welcome to Stewart Metal

Stewart Metal. started its operation in Morbi, in the year 2015. Stewart Metal is leading manufacturer and exporter of Aluminium Bottles, Aluminium Canister, Aluminium Cans and Aluminium Containers ranging from 10 ml to 1 Liters with exports to more than 26 countries. These Aluminium Bottles and Cans are widely used in packing Pesticide, chemical, Essential Oils, Perfumery, Flavors and Fragrance, Pharmaceutical, Agrochemicals and Cosmetic industry..
